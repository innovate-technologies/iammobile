I am mobile

We are crowd sourcing a database of mobile (3G/4G/LTE/HSDPA/UTMS/EDGE/...) connections. And you can help us out by opening this page on your phone on a mobile connections and submitting your entry

Get started

  1. Take your phone out
  2. Go to https://iammobile.shoutca.st
  3. Make sure WiFi it turned off
  4. Press the "I Am Mobile" button
